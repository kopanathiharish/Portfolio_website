Angla's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon rolls


Ingredients


For the dough:

  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter
    1. first

Instuctions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Me the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere here to rise for 2 hours.
